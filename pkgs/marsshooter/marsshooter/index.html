M.A.R.S. - a ridiculous shooter is a 2D space shooter with awesome visual effects and attractive physics. Players can battle each other or computer controlled enemies in exciting game modes: * awesome 2D-graphics with an unique style * a stunning amount of particles * single- and multi-player-support * an artificial intelligence using an aggro-system, which reacts differently upon varying situations * many impressive weapons * customizable ships * a very sexy GUI * several game modes: Space-ball, TeamDeathmatch, Cannonkeep, Deathmatch, Grave-Itation Pit
