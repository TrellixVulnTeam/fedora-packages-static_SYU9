librealsense

Cross-platform camera capture for Intel RealSense

  • Builds

The Intel RealSense SDK is a cross-platform library (Linux, OSX, Windows) for capturing data from the Intel RealSense D400 and SR 300 depth cameras. For older devices (F200, R200, LR200, ZR300), please use librealsense1.
