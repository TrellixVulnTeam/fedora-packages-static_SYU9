The Mondulkiri font families provide Unicode support for the Khmer script. “Mondulkiri” and “Ratanakiri” are the names of two provinces in north-eastern Cambodia, Busra and Oureang are names of places in Mondulkiri province. Khmer Busra dict has alternative shapes for four of the Khmer letters as they are found in some dictionaries.
