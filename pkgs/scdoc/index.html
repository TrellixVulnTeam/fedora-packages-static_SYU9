scdoc

Tool for generating roff manual pages

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

scdoc is a tool designed to make the process of writing man pages more friendly. It reads scdoc syntax from stdin and writes roff to stdout, suitable for reading with man.
