ghc-shakespeare

A toolkit for making compile-time interpolated templates

  • Builds

  • Koschei

Shakespeare is a family of type-safe, efficient template languages. Shakespeare templates are expanded at compile-time, ensuring that all interpolated variables are in scope. Variables are interpolated according to their type through a typeclass. Shakespeare templates can be used inline with a quasi-quoter or in an external file. See the documentation at <http://www.yesodweb.com/book/shakespearean-templates> for more details.
