Slam Soccer 2006 is a funny football game in 3D-comic-style - and it's for free! * Freeware and open source * Funny 3d-comic-style * Enthralling stadium atmosphere * Keyboard and gamepad control * 2-player mode * Career and world cup * Register in the online hall of fame * Build your own stadium * 80 teams * 20 stadiums * 10 weather conditions * 50 adboards * 10 referees * 9 commentators (5 German, 2 English, 2 French) * 3 languages: German, English, French
