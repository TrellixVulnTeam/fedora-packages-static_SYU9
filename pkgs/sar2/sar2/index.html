Search and Rescue II is a rescue helicopter simulator for Linux. It features several missions in which the player pilots a helicopter in order to rescue people in distress. There are several scenarios and helicopter models. Search and Rescue II has a strong focus on realistic physics and low graphics requirements. It is a fork of the game "Search and Rescue" (http://searchandrescue.sf.net), originally developed by Wolfpack Entertainment.
