perl-Math-Expression-Evaluator

Parses, compiles and evaluates mathematics expressions

Math::Expression::Evaluator is a parser, compiler and interpreter for mathematical expressions. It can handle normal arithmetic (including powers wit ^ or **), built-in functions like sin() and variables.
