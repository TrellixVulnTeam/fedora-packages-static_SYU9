mozilla-googlesharing

Anonymizing proxy service for Googlesharing system

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

This mozilla add-on aims to provide a level of anonymity from Google. Prevents google from tracking your searches, movements,and websites you visit. Leaves non-Google traffic completely untouched and unaffected. Also makes this system,completely transparent to the end user.
