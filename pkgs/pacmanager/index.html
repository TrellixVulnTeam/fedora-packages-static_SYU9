pacmanager

Perl Auto Connector a multi-purpose SSH/terminal connection manager

  • Bodhi

PAC is a telnet/ssh/rsh/etc connection manager/automator written in Perl GTK aimed at making administration easier. Users who may have used SecureCRT, PuTTY, and/or mRemoteNG in the past may find this application useful.
