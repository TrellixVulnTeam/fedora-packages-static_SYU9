gdcalc is a financial, statistics, scientific and programmers calculator for gtk+-based under Unix and Linux. It has both Algebraic notation (ie. conventional, TI or Casio-like) and Reverse Polish Notation (HP-style). To customise for fonts & colours: mkdir ~/.config/gdcalc cp /etc/gdcalc/gdcalc.css ~/.config/gdcalc/ This package includes the original dcalc for curses (Unix console) If you want to know more about RPN calculators (and why they are more intuitive than algebraic calculators with their = sign) take a look at http://www.hpcalc.org
