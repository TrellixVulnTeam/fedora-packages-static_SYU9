Haskell OAuth2 authentication client. Tested with the following services: * AzureAD: <https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/active-directory/develop/v1-protocols-oauth-code> * Google: <https://developers.google.com/accounts/docs/OAuth2WebServer> * Github: <http://developer.github.com/v3/oauth/> * Facebook: <http://developers.facebook.com/docs/facebook-login/> * Fitbit: <http://dev.fitbit.com/docs/oauth2/> * StackExchange: <https://api.stackexchange.com/docs/authentication> * DropBox: <https://www.dropbox.com/developers/reference/oauth-guide> * Weibo: <http://open.weibo.com/wiki/Oauth2> * Douban: <http://developers.douban.com/wiki/?title=oauth2>.
