digikam

A digital camera accessing & photo management application

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

digiKam is an easy to use and powerful digital photo management application, which makes importing, organizing and manipulating digital photos a "snap". An easy to use interface is provided to connect to your digital camera, preview the images and download and/or delete them. digiKam built-in image editor makes the common photo correction a simple task.
