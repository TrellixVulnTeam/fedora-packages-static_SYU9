An LV2 drum sequencer

lv2-fabla is a drum sampler plugin instrument. It is ideal for loading up your favorite sampled sounds and bashing away on a MIDI controller. Or if it’s crafty beat programming your after that’s cool too! The ADSR envelope allows the shaping of hi-hats and kicks while the compressor beefs up the sound for those thumping kicks! Additional presets can be found at: https://github.com/harryhaaren/openAV-presets
