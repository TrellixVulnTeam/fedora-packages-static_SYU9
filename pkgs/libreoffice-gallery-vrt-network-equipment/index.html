A network equipment shape gallery for LibreOffice. The gallery includes * Clients & Displays: desktop, thin client, laptop, tablets, phones, industrial panel PCs, wide-screen TV and projector systems. * Peripherals: Just printers and fax for now. * Servers: a range of tower, rack and industrial PCs with emblems for a range of server roles - mix-n-match to suit (the tower can also be used with the thin client to create a desktop tower). * Network & Power: Infrastructure for your network, including industrial fibre/Ethernet/serial components and odds and ends for wireless and mesh networking, solar systems and UPS. * Sensors & Controllers: PLCs & remote I/O, RTUs, data loggers, electricity, water and gas meters, CCTV.
