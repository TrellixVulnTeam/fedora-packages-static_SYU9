Generate stack traces with lexical variables

Devel::StackTrace is pretty good at generating stack traces. PadWalker is pretty good at the inspection and modification of your callers' lexical variables. Devel::StackTrace::WithLexicals is pretty good at generating stack traces with all your callers' lexical variables.
