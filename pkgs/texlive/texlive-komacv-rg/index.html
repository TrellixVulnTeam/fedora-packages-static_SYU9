The komacv-rg bundle provides packages that aid in creating CVs based on the komacv class and creating related documents, such as cover letters and cover sheets for job applications. Concretely, the bundle consists of three packages: komacv-addons, komacv-lco, and komacv-multilang. komacv-addons is a small collection of add-ons and fixes for the komacv class; komacv-lco enables the use of letter class options from scrlttr2 also in komacv-based and other non-scrlttr2-based documents; komacv-multilang enables the provisioning of CVs in multiple languages and the selection of a language via babel or polyglossia.
