Lorem ipsum is an improper Latin filler dummy text, cf. the lipsum package. It is commonly used for demonstrating the textual elements of a document template. Lorum ipse is a Hungarian variation of Lorem ipsum. (Lorum is a Hungarian card game, and ipse is a Hungarian slang word meaning bloke.) With this package you can typeset 150 paragraphs of Lorum ipse. All paragraphs are taken with permission from http://www.lorumipse.hu. Thanks to Lorum Ipse Lab (Viktor Nagy and David Takacs) for their work.
