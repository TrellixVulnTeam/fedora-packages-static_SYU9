Aegyptus contains an Extended List of 7062 Egyptian Hieroglyphs, in regular and bold font weights. There is no standard for Egyptian Hieroglyphs or Meroitic, so they are allocated in the Supplementary Private Use Plane 15. The fonts also cover Basic Latin and some Punctuation and other Symbols. They were created by George Douros, mainly based on the book Hieroglyphica, PIREI IÂ², 2000 and the work of Alan Gardiner.
