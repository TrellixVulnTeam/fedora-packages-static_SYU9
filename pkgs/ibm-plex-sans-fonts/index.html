IBM wanted Plex to be a distinctive, yet timeless workhorse — an alternative to its previous corporate font family, “Helvetica Neue”, for this new era. The Grotesque style was the perfect fit. Not only do Grotesque font families balance human and rational elements, the Grotesque style also came about during the Industrial Age, when IBM was born. This package provides the grotesque sans-serif variable-width IBM Plex Sans, the main font family of the Plex set.
