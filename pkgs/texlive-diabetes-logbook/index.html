texlive-diabetes-logbook Subpackage of texlive

A logbook for people with type one diabetes

  • Bodhi

  • SCM

Initally a logbook for me, a person with type one diabetes mellitus, this evolved over time into a TeX project, making it prettier and/or easier to use. I've made it simple to use, while not forgoing the document's beauty or the speed of input. The logbook, with slight commenting out and editing, could be used as a journal by anybody, although the template and graphing functionality are set up for people using insulin injections and blood glucose teststrips, as well as counting/estimating carbs, protein, and fat. Note: The names of the package's files are not constructed using the long "diabetes-logbook", but the acronym "dmlb" (for "diabetes mellitus log book").
