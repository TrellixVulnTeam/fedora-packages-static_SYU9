perl-MooseX-Role-XMLRPC-Client

Provide the needed bits to be a XML-RPC client

This is a Moose role that provides the methods and attributes needed to enable a class to serve as an XML-RPC client. It is parameterized through MooseX::Role::Parameterized, so you can customize how it embeds in your class. You can even embed it multiple times with different paramaterization, if it strikes your fancy :-)
