BDD Assertions for PHPUnit and Codeception This is very tiny wrapper for PHPUnit assertions, that are aimed to make tests a bit more readable. With BDD assertions influenced by Chai, Jasmine, and RSpec your assertions would be a bit closer to natural language. Autoloader: /usr/share/php/Codeception/autoload-verify.php
