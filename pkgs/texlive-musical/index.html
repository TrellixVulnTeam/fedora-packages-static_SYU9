texlive-musical Subpackage of texlive

Typeset (musical) theatre scripts

This package is designed to simplify the development and distribution of scripts for theatrical musicals, especially ones under development. The output is formatted to follow generally accepted script style[1] while also maintaining a high level of typographic integrity, and includes commands for dialog, lyrics, stage directions, music and dance cues, rehearsal marks, and more. It gracefully handles dialog that crosses page breaks, and can generate lists of songs and lists of dances in the show. [1] There are lots of references for the One True Way to format a script. Naturally, none of them agree.
