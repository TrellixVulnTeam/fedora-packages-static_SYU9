perl-Lingua-EN-Numbers

Turn "407" into "four hundred and seven", etc

Lingua::EN::Numbers turns numbers into English text. It exports (upon request) two functions, num2en and num2en_ordinal. Each takes a scalar value and returns a scalar value. The return value is the English text expressing that number; or if what you provided wasn't a number, then they return undefined.
