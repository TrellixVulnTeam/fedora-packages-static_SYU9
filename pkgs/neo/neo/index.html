neo

Digital rain in your terminal

neo recreates the digital rain effect known from the film "The Matrix". Streams of random characters will endlessly scroll down your terminal screen. neo handles Unicode, 16/256 and 32-bit color. It has automatic detection for terminal color, and supports resizing gracefully.
