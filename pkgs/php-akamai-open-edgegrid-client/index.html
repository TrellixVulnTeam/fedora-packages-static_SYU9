Akamai {OPEN} EdgeGrid Authentication [1] Client for PHP This library implements the Akamai {OPEN} EdgeGrid Authentication scheme on top of Guzzle [2], as both a drop-in replacement client, and middleware. For more information visit the Akamai {OPEN} Developer Community [3]. Autoloader: /usr/share/php/Akamai/Open/EdgeGrid/autoload-client.php [1] https://developer.akamai.com/introduction/Client_Auth.html [2] https://github.com/guzzle/guzzle [3] https://developer.akamai.com/
