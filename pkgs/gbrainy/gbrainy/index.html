gbrainy is a brain teaser game and trainer to have fun and to keep your brain trained. It provides the following types of games: * Logic puzzles. Games designed to challenge your reasoning and thinking skills. * Mental calculation. Games based on arithmetical operations designed to prove your mental calculation skills. * Memory trainers. Games designed to challenge your short term memory.
