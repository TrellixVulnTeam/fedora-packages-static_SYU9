The MgOpen fonts are a font family that includes Latin and Greek glyphs. The fonts have been released under a liberal license, similar to the license covering the Bitstream Vera fonts. This package contains the MgOpen Canonica serif variable-stroke-width typeface, which is based on the design of Times Roman.
