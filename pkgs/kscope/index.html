KScope is a QT5 front-end to Cscope. It provides a source-editing environment for large C projects, such as the Linux kernel. KScope is by no means intended to be a replacement to any of the leading Linux/KDE IDEs, such as KDevelop. First of all, it is not an Integrated Development Environment: it does not provide the usual write/compile/debug cycle supported by most IDE's. Instead, KScope is focused on source editing and analysis.
