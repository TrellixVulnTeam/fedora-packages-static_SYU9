Stellar data set for use by the StarPlot tool from the [Yale] Bright Star Catalog, 5th Rev. Ed. (preliminary), Hoffleit and Warren, 1991. The data set was obtained from the archives of the Astronomical Data Center (ADC) at NASA Goddard Space Flight Center.
