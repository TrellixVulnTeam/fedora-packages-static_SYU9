eog-plugins

A collection of plugins for the eog image viewer

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

It's a collection of plugins for use with the Eye of GNOME Image Viewer. The included plugins provide a map view for where the picture was taken, display of Exif information, Zoom to fit, etc.
