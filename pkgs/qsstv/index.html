qsstv

Qt-based slow-scan TV and fax

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

Qsstv is a program for receiving slow-scan television and fax. These are modes used by hamradio operators. Qsstv uses a soundcard to send and receive images.
