kdegames3

KDE 3 Games not ported to KDE 4

  • Bodhi

Games and gaming libraries for KDE which have not been ported to KDE 4 yet. Included with this package are: atlantik, kasteroids, kbackgammon, kboxpush, kenolaba, kfouleggs, kpoker, ksirtet, ksmiletris, ksnakerace.
