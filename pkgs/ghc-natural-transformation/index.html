ghc-natural-transformation

A natural transformation package

A natural transformation transforms a container 'f a' into another container 'g a'. Natural transformations act as functor morphisms in category theory. The naming of '~>', ':~>' and '$$' were taken, with permission, from Edward Kmett's 'indexed' package.
