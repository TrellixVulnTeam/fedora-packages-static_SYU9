Segment16B, a font mimicking 16-segment digital displays

Segment16A, B and C are three font families, each containing a regular, bold, italic and bold italic version, based on sixteen segment displays. In Segment 16B, the diagonal segments extend to the center, but stop inside the outer segments. This may resemble some newer LCD or LED displays.
