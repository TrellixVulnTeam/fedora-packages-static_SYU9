libpano13

Library for manipulating panoramic images

Helmut Dersch's Panorama Tools library. Provides very high quality manipulation, correction and stitching of panoramic photographs. Due to patent restrictions, this library has a maximum fisheye field-of-view restriction of 179 degrees to prevent stitching of hemispherical photographs.
