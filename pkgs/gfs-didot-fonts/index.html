gfs-didot-fonts

GFS Didot, a 20th century Greek font family

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

Didot’s type was the base for a new font, GFS Didot (1994) which was designed by Takis Katsoulidis, and digitized by George Matthiopoulos. The typeface is accompanied by a matching Latin alphabet based on Hermann Zapf’s PaLatino.
