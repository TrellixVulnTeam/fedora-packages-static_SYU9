poezio

IRC-like jabber (XMPP) console client

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

A jabber (XMPP) console client that aims at being similar to popular IRC clients such as Irssi or Weechat. Its main goal is to let the user connect to the Jabber network and join chat rooms without requiring any registration.
