fastx_toolkit-galaxy Subpackage of fastx_toolkit

Integrate fastx_toolkit with a local Galaxy installation

These files allow the integration of fastx_toolkit with a local installation of Galaxy (http://main.g2.bx.psu.edu/), a web-based platform for analyzing multiple alignments, comparing genomic annotations, profiling metagenomic samples and much more.
