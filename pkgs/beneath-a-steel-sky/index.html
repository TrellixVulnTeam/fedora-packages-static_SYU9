After the Dungeons and Dragons fantasy setting of Revolution's first game, Lure of the Temptress, Revolution decided to go down a completely different avenue with its second adventure game, Beneath a Steel Sky, that of Science Fiction. A bleak vision of the future was imagined, where mind control and medical science combined forces to repress the populace. Leading comic artist, Dave Gibbons, joined the design team to visualise this desperate landscape. The result is the cult classic Beneath a Steel Sky. Notice that this package contains the floppy version, the CD version is also available in the beneath-a-steel-sky-cd package. The CD version contains additional / longer cutscenes and voice acting, but also is much larger: the CD version ways in at 70 MB where as this version is only 8 MB.
