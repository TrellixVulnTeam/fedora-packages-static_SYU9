genius

An arbitrary precision integer and multiple precision floatingpoint calculator

  • Builds
  • Updates

Genius is a calculator program similar in some aspects to BC, Matlab or Maple. GEL is the name of its extension language, in fact, a large part of the standard genius functions are written in GEL itself.
