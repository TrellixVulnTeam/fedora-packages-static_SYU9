Set a century and a half after nuclear war, in this game you explore a world where various factions compete to determine the future of the human race. Features include random plot generation and over two hundred mecha designs. Pilot a giant robot, a city smashing tank, a living jet fighter, or anything else that can be built using the game's sophisticated design system.
