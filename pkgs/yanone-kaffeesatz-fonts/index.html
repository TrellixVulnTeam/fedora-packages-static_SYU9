Yanone Kaffeesatz is a sans-serif decorative Latin font family by Jan Gerner, suitable for titles and short runs of text. Its Bold is reminiscent of 1920s coffee house typography, while the rather thin fonts bridge the gap to present times. You can witness Kaffeesatz use on German fresh-water gyms, Dubai mall promos and New Zealand McDonalds ads. And of course on coffee and foodstuff packaging and caf√© design around the globe.
