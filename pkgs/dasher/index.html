Dasher is an information-efficient text-entry interface, driven by natural continuous pointing gestures. Dasher is a competitive text-entry system wherever a full-size keyboard cannot be used, e.g. when operating a computer one-handed, by joystick, touchscreen, trackball, or mouse, when operating a computer without hands (i.e. by head-mouse or by eyetracker), or on palmtops or wearable computers.
