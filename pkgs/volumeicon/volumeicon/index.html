volumeicon

Lightweight volume control for the system tray

Volume Icon aims to be a lightweight volume control that sits in your system tray. Features: * Change volume by scrolling on the systray icon * Ability to choose which channel to control * Configurable stepsize * Several icon themes * Configurable external mixer * Volume slider * Hotkey support
