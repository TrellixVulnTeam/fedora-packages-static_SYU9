Caladea is a free modern, friendly serif font family based on Cambo (https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Cambo) Designed by Carolina Giovagnoli and Andrés Torresi for Huerta Tipográfica in 2012. Cambo is a modern Latin typeface inspired by the contrast, style and ornaments of Khmer typefaces and writing styles. Its main objective is to be used to write Latin texts in a Khmer context, but it is also an elegant choice for all kinds of texts. Caladea is metric-compatible with Cambria font.
