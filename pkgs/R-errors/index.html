Support for measurement errors in R vectors, matrices and arrays: automatic uncertainty propagation and reporting. Documentation about 'errors' is provided in the paper by Ucar, Pebesma & Azcorra (2018, ), included in this package as a vignette; see 'citation("errors")' for details.
