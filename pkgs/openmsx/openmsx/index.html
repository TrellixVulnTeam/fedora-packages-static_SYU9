openMSX is an emulator for the MSX home computer system. Its goal is to emulate all aspects of the MSX with high accuracy. In addition to emulating MSX, MSX2, MSX2+, MSX Turbo R and many of it's peripherals, it also support emulating the ColecoVision game console and the SpectraVideo SVI-318 and SVI-328 home computer systems.
