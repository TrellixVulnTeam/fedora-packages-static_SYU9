xgrav

A simple physics simulation for a large number of particles

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

X-Grav simulates the effect of gravity, collisions, heat dissipation and a simple chemical reaction. The simulation is in no way meant to be realistic but rather a toy with which you can create stars, planets and even simple solar systems.
