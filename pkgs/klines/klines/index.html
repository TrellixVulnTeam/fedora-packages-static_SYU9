The goal of Kolor Lines is quite plain. The player has to move the colored balls around the game board, gathering them into the lines of the same color by five. Once the line is complete it is removed from the board, therefore freeing precious space. In the same time the new balls keep arriving by three after each move, filling up the game board. Kolor Lines cannot be won, and is played against the high score exclusively. The game ends once the whole game board is filled up with balls
