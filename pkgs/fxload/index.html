fxload

A helper program to download firmware into FX and FX2 EZ-USB devices

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

This program is conveniently able to download firmware into FX and FX2 EZ-USB devices, as well as the original AnchorChips EZ-USB. It is intended to be invoked by udev scripts when the unprogrammed device appears on the bus.
