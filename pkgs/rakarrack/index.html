rakarrack

Audio effects processing rack for guitar

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

Rakarrack is a basic rack of effects for guitar. 10 effects. Two EQ (multi-band and parametric), distortion, overdrive, echo, chorus, Flanger, Phaser, compression and Reverb. Real time processing. JACK support. Online tuner. Bank & Preset management. Most of the effects are based on the magnificent work done by Paul Nasca Octavian in ZynAddSubFX synthesizer. The compressor is based on ArtsCompressor of Matthias Kretzer & Stefen Westerfeld. The tuner was adapted from Tuneit, a tuner in text mode created by Mario Lang. Paul Nasca is our hero and a continuous inspiration
