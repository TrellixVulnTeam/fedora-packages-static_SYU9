gap-pkg-gbnp

Computing Gröbner bases of noncommutative polynomials

  • Bodhi

  • Koji
  • SCM

GBNP provides GAP algorithms for computing Gröbner bases of non-commutative polynomials with coefficients from a field implemented in GAP, and some variations, such as a weighted and truncated version and a tracing facility. The word algorithm is interpreted loosely: in general one cannot expect such an algorithm to terminate, as it would imply solvability of the word problem for finitely presented (semi)groups.
