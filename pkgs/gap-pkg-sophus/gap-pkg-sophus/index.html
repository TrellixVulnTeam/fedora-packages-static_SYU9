The Sophus package is written to compute with nilpotent Lie algebras over finite prime fields. Using this package, you can compute the cover, the list of immediate descendants, and the automorphism group of such Lie algebras. You can also test if two such Lie algebras are isomorphic. The immediate descendant function of the package can be used to classify small-dimensional nilpotent Lie algebras over a given field. For instance, the package author obtained a classification of nilpotent Lie algebras with dimension at most 9 over F_2; see http://www.sztaki.hu/~schneider/Research/SmallLie.
