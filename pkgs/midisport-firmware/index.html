midisport-firmware

Firmware for the M-Audio/Midiman USB MIDI and Audio devices

This package contains the firmware for M-Audio/Midiman USB MIDI & Audio devices. Supported devices: - MidiSport 1x1 - MidiSport 2x2 - MidiSport 4x4 - MidiSport 8x8 - MidiSport Uno - Keystation - Oxygen - Radium (You do not need a firmware download for the USB Audio Quattro, Duo, or MidiSport 2x4.)
