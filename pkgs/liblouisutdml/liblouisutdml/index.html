liblouisutdml

Braille transcription library for UTDML documents

  • Builds

  • Sources

  • Koschei

This is a library intended to provide complete braille transcription services for UTDML (Unified Tactile Document Markup Language) documents. It translates into appropriate braille codes and formats according to its style sheet and the specifications in the document. liblouisutdml is the successor of liblouisxml.
