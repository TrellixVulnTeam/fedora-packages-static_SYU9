Gaupol is an editor for text-based subtitle files. It helps you with tasks such as creating and translating subtitles, timing subtitles to match video and correcting common errors. Gaupol includes a built-in video player and also supports launching an external one. Gaupol is available for Linux and Windows, released as Free Software under the GNU General Public License (GPL).
