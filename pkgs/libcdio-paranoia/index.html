libcdio-paranoia

CD paranoia on top of libcdio

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

This CDDA reader distribution ('libcdio-cdparanoia') reads audio from the CDROM directly as data, with no analog step between, and writes the data to a file or pipe as .wav, .aifc or as raw 16 bit linear PCM. Split off from libcdio to allow more flexible licensing and to be compatible with cdparanoia-III-10.2's license. And also, libcdio is just too large.
