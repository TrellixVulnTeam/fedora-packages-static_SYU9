Écolier Lignes Court, a schoolchildren cursive Latin font family with lines

The Écolier court font families were created by Jean-Marie Douteau to mimic the traditional cursive writing French children are taught in school. He kindly released two of them under the OFL, which are redistributed in this package. The « lignes » (lines) Écolier Court font variant includes the Seyes lining commonly used on schoolchildren notepads.
