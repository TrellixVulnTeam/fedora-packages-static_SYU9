ochusha

A GTK+ 2ch.net BBS Browser

The ochusha is BBS, especially 2ch.net, browser with GUI. It uses the GTK+ toolkit for all of its interface needs. The ochusha offers a sort of features such as multi-level popup view of `response's, embeded and popup view of images that helps users to interact with BBSs.
