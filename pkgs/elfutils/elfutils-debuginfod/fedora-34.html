2021-02-08 Mark Wielaard <mjw at fedoraproject dot org> - 0.183-1 - Upgrade to upstream 0.183 - debuginfod: New thread-busy metric and more detailed error metrics. New --fdcache-mintmp and tracking of filesystem freespace. - debuginfod-client: DEBUGINFOD_SONAME macro added to debuginfod.h can be used to dlopen the libdebuginfod.so library. New function debuginfod_set_verbose_fd and DEBUGINFOD_VERBOSE environment variable. - config: profile.sh and profile.csh won't export DEBUGINFOD_URLS unless configured --enable-debuginfod-urls[=URLS] - elflint, readelf: Recognize SHF_GNU_RETAIN.
