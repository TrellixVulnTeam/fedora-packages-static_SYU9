gnokii

Linux/Unix tool suite for various mobile phones

  • Builds

Gnokii provides tools and a user space driver for use with mobile phones under Linux, various unices and Win32. With gnokii you can do such things as make data calls, update your address book, change calendar entries, send and receive SMS messages and load ring tones depending on the phone you have.
