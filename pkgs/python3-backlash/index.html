python3-backlash Subpackage of python-backlash

Standalone WebOb port of the Werkzeug Debugger with Python3 support meant to replace WebError in TurboGears2

backlash is a standalone version of the Werkzeug Debugger based on WebOb adapted to support for Python3. backlash has born as a future replacement for WebError in upcoming TurboGears2 versions.
