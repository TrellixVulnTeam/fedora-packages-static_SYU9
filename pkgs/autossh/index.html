autossh

Utility to autorestart SSH tunnels

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji

autossh is a utility to start and monitor an ssh tunnel. If the tunnel dies or stops passing traffic, autossh will automatically restart it.
