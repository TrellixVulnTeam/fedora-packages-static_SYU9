gap-pkg-cohomolo

Cohomology groups of finite groups on finite modules

This package may be used to perform certain cohomological calculations on a finite permutation group G. The following properties of G can be computed: 1. The p-part Mul_p of the Schur multiplier Mul of G, and a presentation of a covering extension of Mul_p by G, for a specified prime p; 2. The dimensions of the first and second cohomology groups of G acting on a finite dimensional KG-module M, where K is a field of prime order; and 3. Presentations of split and nonsplit extensions of M by G.
