perl-CatalystX-REPL

Read-eval-print-loop for debugging your Catalyst application

  • Koschei

Using Carp::REPL with a Catalyst application is hard. That's because of all the internal exceptions that are being thrown and caught by Catalyst during application startup. You'd have to manually skip over all of those. This role works around that by automatically setting up Carp::REPL after starting your application, if the CATALYST_REPL or MYAPP_REPL environment variables are set.
