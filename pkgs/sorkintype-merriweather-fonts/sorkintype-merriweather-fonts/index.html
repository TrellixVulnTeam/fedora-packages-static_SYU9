Merriweather, a warm space-saving serif font family

Merriweather offers a Renaissance warmth while using proportions which are space-saving. It is suitable for editorial design, news and other kinds of space sensitive typography. Merriweather was designed to be a text face that is pleasant to read on screens. It features a very large x height, slightly condensed letter-forms, a mild diagonal stress, sturdy serifs and open forms
