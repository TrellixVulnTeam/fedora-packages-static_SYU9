almanah

Application for keeping an encrypted diary

Almanah Diary is a small application to ease the management of an encrypted personal diary. It's got good editing abilities, including text formatting and printing. Evolution tasks and appointments will be listed to ease the creation of diary entries related to them. At the same time, you can create diary entries using multiple events.
