KGuitar is a powerful KDE-based music tabulature editor with support of multiple guitar (or any fretted instrument) and drum tracks. KGuitar also supports classic note scores, MIDI synthesizer output, chord, scales, modes, melody and rhythm construction and analysis tools, lots of tab effects, lyrics, Guitar Pro files import and lots of other things.
