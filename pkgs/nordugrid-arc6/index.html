NorduGrid is a collaboration aiming at development, maintenance and support of the middleware, known as the Advanced Resource Connector (ARC). The ARC middleware is a software solution that uses distributed computing technologies to enable sharing and federation of computing resources across different administrative and application domains. ARC is used to create distributed infrastructures of various scope and complexity, from campus to national and global deployments.
