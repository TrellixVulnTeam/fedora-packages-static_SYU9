The Linux Director Daemon (ldirectord) was written by Jacob Rief. ldirectord is a stand alone daemon for monitoring the services on real servers. Currently, HTTP, HTTPS, and FTP services are supported. ldirectord is simple to install and works with Pacemaker (http://clusterlabs.org/). See 'ldirectord -h' and linux-ha/doc/ldirectord for more information.
