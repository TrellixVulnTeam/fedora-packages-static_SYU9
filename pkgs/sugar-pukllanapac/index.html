Pukllanapac is a sliding puzzle game; the objective is to rearrange tiles so that all of the circles (and semicircles) are composed of sectors of the same color. There are three different patterns: circles, triangles and hexagons. Drag tiles to swap their position; click on tiles to rotate them.
