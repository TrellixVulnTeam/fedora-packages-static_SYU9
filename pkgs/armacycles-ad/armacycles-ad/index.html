armacycles-ad

A lightcycle game in 3D

  • Koschei

In this game you ride a lightcycle; that is a sort of motorbike that cannot be stopped and leaves a wall behind it. The main goal of the game is to make your opponents' lightcycles crash into a wall while avoiding the same fate. The focus of the game lies on the multiplayer mode, but it provides challenging AI opponents for a quick training match.
