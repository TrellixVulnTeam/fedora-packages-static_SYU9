php-Monolog

Sends your logs to files, sockets, inboxes, databases and various web services

Monolog sends your logs to files, sockets, inboxes, databases and various web services. Special handlers allow you to build advanced logging strategies. This library implements the PSR-3 [1] interface that you can type-hint against in your own libraries to keep a maximum of interoperability. You can also use it in your applications to make sure you can always use another compatible logger at a later time. [1] http://www.php-fig.org/psr/psr-3/
