dblatex is a program that transforms your SGML/XMLDocBook documents to DVI, PostScript or PDF by translating them into pure LaTeX as a first process. MathML 2.0 markups are supported, too. It started as a clone of DB2LaTeX. Authors: -------- Beno√Æt Guillon Andreas Hoenen
