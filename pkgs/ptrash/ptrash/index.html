ptrash

Move file(s) to $XDG_DATA_HOME/Trash directory

Ptrash moves the named file(s) to the Trash directory. Trash is located under $XDG_DATA_HOME directory as defined by the Trash specification.[*] It is a simple console based utility, I wrote after deleting some files, which I couldn't retrieve back. Ptrash can also restore file(s) back to there original location. [*] https://standards.freedesktop.org/trash-spec/trashspec-latest.html
