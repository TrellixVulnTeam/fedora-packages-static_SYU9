wmMatrix

DockApp version of Jamie Zawinski's xmatrix screensaver hack

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

wmMatrix displays The Matrix (from the film of the same name) in a Window Maker dock application. Based on the xscreensaver module created by Jamie Zawinski. Although it works best with Window Maker, wmMatrix also works fine with other window managers.
