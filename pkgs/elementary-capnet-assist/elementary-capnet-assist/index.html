elementary-capnet-assist

Captive Portal Assistant for elementary

  • Koschei

Assists users in connective to Captive Portals such as those found on public access points in train stations, coffee shops, universities, etc. Upon detection, the assistant appears showing the captive portal. Once a connection is known to have been established, it dismisses itself. Written in Vala and using WebkitGtk+.
