njam

Maze-game, eat all the cookies while avoiding the badguys

Njam is a fast-paced maze-game where you must eat all the cookies while avoiding the badguys. Special cookies give you the power to freeze or eat the bad guys. The game features single and multiplayer modes, network play, duelling and cooperative games, great music and sound effects, customizable level skins, many different levels and an integrated level editor.
