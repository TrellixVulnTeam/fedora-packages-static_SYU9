t-prot

A filter which improves the readability of email messages and Usenet posts

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

t-prot (TOFU Protection) is a filter which improves the readability of email messages and Usenet posts by hiding some of their annoying parts. The annoyances it handles include mailing list footers, signatures, TOFU, sequences of blank lines, and repeated punctuation.
