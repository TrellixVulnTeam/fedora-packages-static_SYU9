padthv1

An old-school polyphonic additive synthesizer

padthv1 is an old-school polyphonic additive synthesizer with stereo effects. padthv1 is based on the PADsynth algorithm by Paul Nasca, as a special variant of additive synthesis. This is the standalone Jack version.
