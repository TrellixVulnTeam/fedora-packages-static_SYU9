sugar-read

A document reader for Sugar

The Read activity allows the laptop to act as a book reader. It has a simple interface, and will view many kinds of text and image-based book- like materials. It will have particular strengths in handheld mode, with extremely low power consumption and simple navigation controls. Read can read PDF files, single-page TIFF files, and also read DJVU files.
