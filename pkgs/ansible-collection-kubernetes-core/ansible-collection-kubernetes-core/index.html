ansible-collection-kubernetes-core provides the kubernetes.core (formerly known as community.kubernetes) Ansible collection. The collection includes a variety of Ansible content to help automate the management of applications in Kubernetes and OpenShift clusters, as well as the provisioning and maintenance of clusters themselves.
