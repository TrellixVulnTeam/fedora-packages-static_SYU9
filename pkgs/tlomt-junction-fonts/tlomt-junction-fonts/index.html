tlomt-junction-fonts

A humanist sans serif font

Designed by Caroline Hadilaksono, inspired by her favorite humanist sans serif typefaces. It has the hand drawn and human qualities of a serif, and still retains the clarity and efficiencies of a sans serif typeface. It combines the best of both worlds. Old style and lining figures are included.
