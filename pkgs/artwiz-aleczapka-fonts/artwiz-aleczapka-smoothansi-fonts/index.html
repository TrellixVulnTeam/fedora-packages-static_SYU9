Artwiz is a family of very small futuristic fonts, with varying styles of typefaces designed at a single pixel size. The minimal nature of the fonts makes them popular with users of lightweight window managers. These fonts have been updated by Alec Zapka to be compatible with modern software and support an extended character set. This package contains the Smoothansi font in three encodings, English, German, and Swedish.
