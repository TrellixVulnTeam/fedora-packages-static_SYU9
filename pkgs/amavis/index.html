amavis

Email filter with virus scanner and spamassassin support

  • Bodhi
  • Bugzilla
  • FAF
  • Koji
  • SCM

amavis is a high-performance and reliable interface between mailer (MTA) and one or more content checkers: virus scanners, and/or Mail::SpamAssassin Perl module. It is written in Perl, assuring high reliability, portability and maintainability. It talks to MTA via (E)SMTP or LMTP, or by using helper programs. No timing gaps exist in the design which could cause a mail loss.
