Rapid Photo Downloader is written by a photographer for professional and amateur photographers. It can download photos from multiple memory cards and Portable Storage Devices simultaneously. It provides a variety of options for sub-folder creation, image renaming and backup. It does not download images directly from a camera unless the camera is recognized as an external drive.
