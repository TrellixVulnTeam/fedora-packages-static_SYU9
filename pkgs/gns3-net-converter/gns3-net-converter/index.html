gns3-net-converter

GNS3 is a graphical network simulator that allows you to design complex network topologies. You may run simulations or configure devices ranging from simple workstations to powerful routers. GNS3 Converter is designed to convert old ini-style GNS3 topologies (<=0.8.7) to the newer version v1+ JSON format for use in GNS3 v1+.
