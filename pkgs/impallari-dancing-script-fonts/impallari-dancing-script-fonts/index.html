Dancing Script is a lively casual script where the letters bounce and change size slightly. Caps are big, and goes below the baseline. Dancing Script references popular scripts typefaces from the 50â€™s. It relates to Murray Hill (Emil Klumpp. 1956) in his weight distribution, and to Mistral (Roger Excoffon. 1953) in his lively bouncing effect. Use it when you want a friendly, informal and spontaneous look.
