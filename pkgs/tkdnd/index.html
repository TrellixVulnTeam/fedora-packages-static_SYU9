Tk Drag & Drop: tkdnd is an extension that adds native drag & drop capabilities to the tk toolkit. It can be used with any tk version equal or greater to 8.4. Under unix the drag & drop protocol in use is the XDND protocol version 4 (also used by the QT toolkit, KDE & GNOME Desktops).
