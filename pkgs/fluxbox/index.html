Fluxbox is yet another window-manager for X. It's based on the Blackbox 0.61.1 code. Fluxbox looks like blackbox and handles styles, colors, window placement and similar thing exactly like blackbox (100% theme/style compatibility). So what's the difference between fluxbox and blackbox then? The answer is: LOTS! Have a look at the homepage for more info ;)
